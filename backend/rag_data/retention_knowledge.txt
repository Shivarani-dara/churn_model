Month-to-month contract customers have higher churn risk. Offer discounted annual or two-year contract plans.

Customers with high monthly charges are price-sensitive. Offer loyalty discounts, plan optimization, or bundle deals.

Customers without online security may perceive lower service value. Offer a free security trial or discounted security bundle.

Customers without tech support may churn because unresolved issues reduce trust. Offer premium support or proactive service checks.

Low-tenure customers are early-stage users and need stronger onboarding, welcome benefits, and proactive support.

Customers with low engagement should receive personalized outreach, feature education, and relevant plan recommendations.

Bundled services can improve retention by increasing convenience and perceived value.

Paperless billing and digital engagement can improve convenience and customer stickiness.
